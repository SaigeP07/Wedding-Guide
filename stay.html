Flight & Stay Information

The prominent airport in the area is the Seattle-Tacoma International Airport, or SEATAC (SEA). Most airlines fly in and out of SEATAC, and there are a multitude of restaurants inside the airport as well as hotels within driving distance. There is an Enterprise Rent-A-Car and a Hertz Rent-A-Car located at the airport for those looking to rent a vehicle.


Listed Below are some average flight prices, per passenger, from multiple air lines so you can begin to look into what would be the best option. All listed pricing is an average for flights arriving on September 12, 2023 originating in Tampa (TPA) and departing on September 15, 2023 from SEATAC.

- Southwest: $333 (Round Trip, 1 Stop)
- Frontier: 295$ (Round trip, 1 Stop)
- United Airlines: $373 (Round trip, 1 stop)
- Jet Blue: $472 (Round Trip, 1 Stop)
- Alaska Airlines: $490 (Round Trip, Nonstop and 1 Stop options)
- American Airlines: $495 (Round Trip, 1 Stop)
- Delta Airlines: $614 (Round Trip, Nonstop and 1 Stop options)

Please account for the time of the layover in you arrival and departure times. For example, the average layover for an American Airlines flight was between 1-2 hours while the average layover for Frontier airlines ranged anywhere from 9 hours to 18 hours.


Each airline also has pros and cons to consider. American Airlines has a great loyalty program as well as lounge access. Delta Airlines have wider and more spacious seating options, more flexibility with tickets and no changing fees, and the first checked bags fees are waived at check in. Southwest is also very flexible, has many flight options, and each passenger gets one free checked bag, as well as an overhead carry on and an under seat personal item.

Below are a few of the nearby cities loacted in-between Seattle and port Angeles - We encourage everyone to do their own research on where you eventually choose to stay, but these are good options that should help with narrowing your search. AirBnB and Google Hotels are great for narrowing down the best deals on a place to stay for your time with us

  • Bainbridge island - car ferry ride over to Seattle. 1 hr 48 min from port Angeles. Good place if spending most of your time exploring Seattle. Best option if not staying directly in Seattle but wanting to spend most of your time there
  • Poulsbo and surrounding areas - right in the middle of Seattle and port Angeles. Good option if you're wanting to spend time in both Seattle and Olympic National park. 1hr 15 min drive both ways. Good hotel options. Small town next to liberty bay. 1hr 12 min from airport.
  • Port Orchard and surrounding areas - 50 min away from airport, 1hr 5 min away from seattle by car (1hr 30min by way of ferry) 1hr 30 away from Port Angeles best option if flying in early and wanting to spend time in seattle + wanting to be a reasonable drive away from where the wedding will be located.
  • Tacoma and surrounding areas - Washingtons other major city. 25 min from SeaTac airport, 35 min to seattle, 2 hr to port angeles for wedding. Good option for those on an extended stay and for those wanting to explore both major cities. Also close to Mt Rainer National Park (1hr 20 min away) which has many breathtaking, easy to access views.
  • Sequim or Port angeles - Small towns (Sequim away from 25min away from port Angeles). Lots of good prices on hotels and inns. good option if only staying for wedding and bar crawl.
