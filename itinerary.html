Itinerary & Ceremony info

We have plans for what we would like to do while in the state, and we encourage all who want and are able to take full advantage of their own trip! We do have a section dedicated to outings, restaurants, sights, and hikes that might interest you, and here is a list of what we plan to do during our trip:

- Monday 9/11: Fly in to SEATAC
- Tuesday 9/12: Sightseeing at Mt. Rainier National Park
- Wednesday 9/13: explore Seattle, Dinner with everyone
- Thursday 9/14: Wedding Day! A specific time line will be curated on this day with the help of our photographer. We will send the full timeline once we have ironed those details out with Sally. As of now, we are planning a golden hour ceremony, which would put us in the park around 4:30 PM-5 PM with a sunset of 7:20 PM. Bar and Pub crawl in downtown Port Angeles that evening
- Friday 9/15: Visiting movie locations and the west coast at Forks and LaPush
- Saturday 9/16: Sightseeing Olympic National Park
- Sunday 9/17: Fly Home


In addition to the wedding on Thursday, we are also inviting everyone to join us for dinner in Seattle in lieu of a traditional rehearsal dinner. We want nothing more than to enjoy the company of everyone we love in one place! We are also wanting festivities to continue the evening of our wedding, by way of a pub crawl in downtown Port Angeles. There are multiple bars, shops, and pubs that we’d like to check out that are all within a short walking distance of each-other. We hope It’ll be a great way for us to enjoy everyone’s company in a casual and fun setting.
