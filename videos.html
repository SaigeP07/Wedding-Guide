We wanted to include some videos we thought would be helpful for planning things you might want to do while you're here. A Note for those wanting to visit the National parks: There are several entrances to each national park and it will be important to plan your trip around which entrance is closest to you. (Trails and Parking overlooks are limited to whichever entrance is nearest to you: more information on helping to plan your park trip can be found at the bottom of this Video Section.)
